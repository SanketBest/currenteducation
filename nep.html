National Education Policy (NEP)

The National Education Policy (NEP) aims to address many of the issues present in the current education system and promote a more balanced and inclusive approach to learning.

Key Benefits of NEP

  • Flexibility in choosing subjects to promote interdisciplinary learning.
  • Focus on holistic, student-centered education rather than rote learning.
  • Incorporation of vocational education from an early stage to enhance practical skills.
  • Emphasis on critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills.
  • Reduction in the curriculum content to allow more time for experiential learning.
