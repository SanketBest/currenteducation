Pros and Cons of Current Education System

Pros

  • Encourages structured learning
  • Provides a benchmark for education quality
  • Prepares students for standardized tests

Cons

  • May stifle creativity and critical thinking
  • Can lead to student disengagement
  • Overemphasis on exam results
